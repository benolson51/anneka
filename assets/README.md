# Photos

Save Anneka's photos here with these exact filenames and they'll appear on the site
automatically (the pages already reference them). Until a file exists, that spot shows
a small caption instead of a broken image.

| Filename | Where it shows | Best photo | Shape |
|---|---|---|---|
| `anneka-hero.jpg` | Home page hero (top right) | Standing in her office doorway under the "Anneka G. Sweeney, Attorney at Law" sign | Vertical (4:5) |
| `anneka-about.jpg` | About page (next to her bio) | Office portrait by the leather chair | Vertical (4:5) |
| `anneka-desk.jpg` | Home page "Hello, I'm Anneka" section | At her desk on the phone / working | Square-ish (will crop to square) |

Tips:
- JPG or PNG both work. Aim for at least ~1000px on the short side; keep files under ~500 KB
  if you can (resize/compress so the site stays fast).
- The Village Mall storefront shot is also great — drop it in as `anneka-kingsburg.jpg`
  if you'd like it added to a "local" section later.

Two ways to get the files live:
1. Save them in this folder, tell Ben, and he'll push them to GitHub, **or**
2. Upload them directly on github.com → the `assets` folder → "Add file" → "Upload files".
   The site redeploys automatically within ~1 minute.
