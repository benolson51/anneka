About

Meet Anneka

A Kingsburg native, born and raised — and an estate planning attorney who believes good planning starts with really listening.

Photo: save as assets/anneka-about.jpg

Counsel you can actually talk to.

I've lived in Kingsburg my whole life. I graduated from Kingsburg High School, went on to Fresno State, and earned my law degree from San Joaquin College of Law. This is my home — these are my neighbors — and that's exactly why I do this work.

I opened The Law Office of Anneka G. Sweeney as a boutique practice focused on estate planning, because I saw how often good people put off planning. It feels intimidating, impersonal, or like something only "wealthy" families need. I wanted to change that — to make estate planning approachable, warm, and genuinely helpful.

When you work with me, you work with me. I'll explain your options in plain language, answer every question without watching the clock, and build a plan that fits your family and your life.
